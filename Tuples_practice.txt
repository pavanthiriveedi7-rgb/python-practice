Set Operations (The "Venn Diagram" Logic) -->Union (|) Combines two sets (all items from both, no duplicates). -->Intersection (&) Finds items present in both sets. -->Difference (-) Items in the first set but not in the second. -->Symmetric Difference (^) Items in either set, but not in both.


Cse_A = {"pavan","vijay","malli","kumar","gouse"}
Cse_B = {"pujith","sai","balaiah","prabhas"}
CSE = Cse_A | Cse_B
print(CSE)
CSE = print(Cse_A & Cse_B)
print(Cse_A - Cse_B)
print(Cse_A ^ Cse_B)


output:



{'balaiah', 'pavan', 'vijay', 'gouse', 'prabhas', 'sai', 'kumar', 'pujith', 'malli'}
set()
